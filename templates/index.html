The Restaurant

Welcome to our Chilean restaurant, influenced by the South American cuisine. As a guest at one of our tables, you enjoy good, well-prepared Chilean food in an inviting environment that radiates warmth, joy and community. Perhaps you've already experienced our chef Eduardo's magical dishes on a previous visit and know what an explosion of flavors you can expect. But for you as a new guest who wonders what is so special about us - visit us and the answer will be yours.

Menu

Experience classic Chilean dishes from north to south of Chile. From classic favorites like empanadas to delicious seafood soups.

Chorillana
French fries, finely cut onion, spicy sausage, beefsteak and, crowning it all, one or two fried eggs.
Pastel de Choclo
French fries, finely cut onion, spicy sausage, beefsteak and, crowning it all, one or two fried eggs
Plato de Empanadas
Plate with empanadas. Popular pastry filled with diced meat/chicken, hard-boiled egg and raisins/olives.
Sopa de Mariscos
Delicious seafood soup made of hake, mussels, onion and garlic with bread slices and parsley on top.
